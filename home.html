My Portfolio

Feed Forward

FeedForward is a mobile app I designed and developed with my team to help reduce food waste by connecting restaurants with people and organizations in need. Restaurants can list surplus food, and users can request, buy at a discount, or even negotiate. I focused on creating a clean, user-friendly interface and seamless experience — from intuitive listings to impact dashboards and volunteer-powered deliveries.

Figma Canva React Native Nodejs MongoDB

Soul Support

Soul Support is a mental health platform I designed and developed with my team to make emotional wellbeing more accessible. It allows users to book one-on-one sessions with certified psychiatrists, join webinars, chat with an AI-powered support bot, and explore a library of wellness blogs. My focus was on building a calm, approachable interface that supports users through every step of their mental health journey.

Prototyping React Nodejs PostgreSQL
